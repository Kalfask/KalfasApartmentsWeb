Discover Nea Michaniona

A vibrant seaside town blending fishing heritage, stunning beaches & local culture.

📌 Quick Facts

Located ~25 km south of Thessaloniki, Nea Michaniona was established in 1923 by refugees from Kyzikos. Today it's known for its rich fishing legacy and the largest fleet in Northern Greece

Top Things to Do

  • 🌊 Relax on the beautiful beaches like Agios Georgios and Nea Michaniona Beach
  • 🍽️ Enjoy fresh seafood at local taverns
  • 🛍️ Explore the vibrant promenade with shops and cafes
  • 🎣 Visit the fishing port and learn about the local fishing industry
  • 🏛️ Discover nearby archaeological sites and cultural landmarks

Festivals & Culture

  • Sardela Festival
  • Feast of Panagia

Local Cuisine

  • Fresh seafood dishes like grilled sardines and octopus
  • Traditional Greek salads with local ingredients
  • Delicious pastries and desserts from local bakeries

Nearby Beaches

  • Potamos Beach - A popular spot with clear waters and beach bars
  • Nea Michaniona Beach - Family-friendly with amenities and shallow waters
  • Epanomi Beach - Known for its scenic views and tranquility
  • Agelohori Beach
  • Neoi Epivates Beach
